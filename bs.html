Important Notes:

  • What does AEO mean for Western Ivey?: It is the conditional pre-admission to the Ivey Program. Most students apply to the Ivey program in their 2nd year, but accepting as a AEO student means guaranteeing a spot on the Ivey program in your 3rd year. Applying for the AEO is a seperate decision than your admission to Western.
  • None of the schools officially publish program-level acceptance rates for these undergrad business programs. The acceptance rates are estimates.
  • Deadlines & fees fluctuate every cycle. Always follow the live OUAC key dates plus each school's admissions portal for the most current information.
  • For Ontario students: The OUAC rule is maximum 3 choices at any single Ontario university. UBC lets you pick two degree choices on one application.
  • Information last verified: September 2025

Ontario Students Courses to Course Code Conversion:

  • Grade 12 English → ENG4U
  • Grade 12 Advanced Functions → MHF4U
  • Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors → MCV4U
  • Grade 12 Mathematics of Data Management → MDM4U

Financial Notes:

    How does the OUAC Financials work?

    You pay $156 for any 3 programs from any Ontario university of your choice. Any additional program you wish to apply to is $50 extra. Some universities may have their own additional fees, such as supplementary application fees or program-specific fees on top of the program application fee.
